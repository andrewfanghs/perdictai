PERDICT.AI

A product of the JARVIS-DHL project

I was born from the AI in Health Grand Challenge to help primary care teams stop or slow the progression and complication development in 3H - Hyperglycemia (diabetes), Hypertension (high blood pressure) and Hyperlipidemia (high cholesterol) - by 20% in 5 years.

I am designed to generate predictive and personalized insights to help improve diabetes care.
